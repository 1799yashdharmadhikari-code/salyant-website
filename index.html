Our Story

In an era where digital transformation has become essential rather than optional, we recognized a critical gap in the market. Organizations across the UK were struggling not with implementing technology, but with making it work sustainably over time.

Founded in 2025, Salyant emerged from a simple belief: enterprise automation should be built to last. We've seen too many ambitious AI projects that dazzle in demos but falter in production. Too many integrations that work brilliantly until they need to scale. Too many systems that lack the governance frameworks enterprises actually need.

Our approach is different. We design for the long term from day one—building systems that scale, adapt, and remain maintainable as your organization evolves. Every automation workflow we create, every integration we build, every AI implementation we deploy is architected with enterprise governance, operational continuity, and sustained performance in mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does Salyant serve?

We work across multiple sectors including financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and professional services. Our enterprise-grade solutions are designed to meet the stringent requirements of regulated industries while remaining flexible enough for organizations of all sizes.

How long does a typical automation project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A focused workflow automation might take 6-12 weeks, while enterprise-wide system integration projects typically run 3-6 months. We provide detailed project plans with clear milestones during our initial discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Absolutely. Long-term operational support is core to our service model. We offer flexible support arrangements including 24/7 monitoring, regular optimization reviews, system enhancements, and dedicated technical support to ensure your automation continues to deliver value as your business evolves.

What makes Salyant different from other AI service providers?

We focus on building systems that last. While many providers excel at proof-of-concepts and demos, we architect for production from day one—with proper governance, security, scalability, and maintainability. Our enterprise mindset means we consider compliance, auditability, and operational continuity in everything we build.

Where is Salyant based?

Salyant is headquartered in London, United Kingdom. We serve organizations across the UK and work with clients who value UK-based delivery teams and data residency within British jurisdictions.
