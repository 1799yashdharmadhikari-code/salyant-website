Common Questions

What industries do you serve? +
We primarily work with regulated sectors including Financial Services, Healthcare, and Manufacturing, where governance and reliability are paramount.
Which business processes can AI automate? +
AI can automate a wide range of processes including data entry, document processing, customer support triage, report generation, and complex approval workflows.
How long does an AI automation project take? +
Initial discovery and strategy take 1-2 weeks. Full implementation and integration typically range from 4 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity of your systems.
What software can Salyant integrate with? +
We integrate with all major enterprise software including CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), ERPs, ITSM platforms (Jira, ServiceNow), and custom legacy databases via robust APIs.
Do you build custom AI agents? +
Yes. We architect bespoke, governed AI agents designed specifically for your operational logic and constrained strictly within your business data boundaries.
Do you offer post-delivery support? +
Yes. Support is core to our model. We offer 24/7 operational support packages to ensure your critical systems remain available and performant.
How does your pricing work? +
We typically operate on a fixed-outcome basis for project delivery, followed by a retainer model for ongoing operational support and continuous improvement.
