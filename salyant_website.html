About SALYANT™

Who We Are

SALYANT is a technology consulting firm founded to bridge the critical digital skills gap facing UK businesses. We deliver enterprise-grade solutions by combining UK-based strategic oversight with global delivery excellence.

Our Mission

To democratize access to world-class technology expertise, enabling businesses of all sizes to compete in the digital economy without prohibitive costs.

Why We Exist

With 75% of UK IT firms struggling to find qualified talent and the digital skills shortage projected to cost £27.6 billion by 2030, businesses need alternative solutions. We provide immediate access to specialized expertise in AI, cybersecurity, cloud, and software development—the exact skills in critical shortage.

Our Approach

We leverage a global delivery model that provides 24/7 coverage, rapid deployment (projects start within 7 days), and 40-60% cost savings compared to traditional UK consultancies—without compromising quality or security.
